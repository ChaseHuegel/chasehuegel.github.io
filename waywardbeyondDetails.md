Wayward Beyond
{: class="console"}

![wayward beyond](/images/library_header.png)

A retro-future sandbox RPG set in a frontier era galaxy with procedurally-generated elements. Create, explore, trade, and battle to stake your claim among the stars of a wild galaxy!

Development has been ongoing since Q4 2025 and a free playtest will be launching soon on Steam. Development will be open to user feedback to help shape the game before moving into Early Access.
